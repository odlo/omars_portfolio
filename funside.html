The Leafy Elephant's Foot is a species of flowering plant that is native to Kentucky. It's a very small plant, with small pink colored flowers, which I found while on a hike in Mammoth's Cave Kentucky. Because I love the name of this plant so much, It is my favorite plant.

Favorite Bug: Snowberry Clearwing

The Snowberry Clearwing, a species of moth (I was surprised too) is a beautifully large pollinator that resembles a hummingbird and a butterfly at the exact same time. It's clear wings and furry, long tail combined with unusually large antennae also make this species known as a "flying lobster." The Snowberry Clearwing is my favortie bug not only because of its unusual beauty, but because of its abilities to imitate something it is not.
